About SymmptoScan

SymptoScan- your health guardian, seamlessly predicting diseases, offering personalized health insights, and fostering a proactive well-being revolution through cutting-edge technology and user-centric design.

Machine Learning Model

The core of “SymptoScan” lies in its machine learning model, meticulously trained to predict 41 types of diseases which are mainly pre-trained model used for implementation of this application from HuggingFace

Disease Suggestion Section

Users are able to input their symptoms and health information into the app, which will then utilize a machine learning model to provide predictions about potential diseases.

Health Education Section

The app features a comprehensive information of diseases, including information about symptoms, risk factors, prevention strategies, and available treatments.

Empowering Early Intervention

Providing users with predictive insights to empower early intervention and preventive measures, transforming the narrative of healthcare from reactive to proactive.

Revolutionizing Disease Detection

Driving a paradigm shift in disease detection, SymptoScan leverages advanced machine learning to predict 41 diseases, revolutionizing the way we approach health diagnostics.

Transformative User Experience

SymptoScan redefines the user experience, delivering not just an app but a transformative tool that prioritizes psychological aspects, creating a supportive health interaction.

Cultivating a Culture of Wellness

Inspiring Informed Decision-Making
